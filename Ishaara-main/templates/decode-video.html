ISL to Text

Record a video of the sign language sentence you want to convert to text. Click on the button on the right to start recording.

There are a few things to keep in mind while recording the video:

  1. Make sure that the your shoulders are always visible
  2. The system will keep recording till you move your hands out of the frame
  3. Move at a reasonable speed or the system will not be able to detect it
  4. Make sure that the lighting is good
  5. The hands and body should be facing the camera while signing
  6. To close the video window, press 'q' after focusing the window
